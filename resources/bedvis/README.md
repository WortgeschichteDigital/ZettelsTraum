
This folder contains a visualization that was created with the aid of [_Zettel’s Traum_](https://github.com/WortgeschichteDigital/ZettelsTraum), the cardbox app of [_Wortgeschichte digital_](https://wortgeschichten.zdl.org/) (WGd).

WGd is a historical dictionary of the Ger­man language that focuses on describing semantic shifts from about 1600 until today. The research project ist hosted by the [_Göttingen Academy of Sciences and Humanities in Lower Saxony_](https://adw-goe.de/) (_Niedersächsische Akademie der Wissenschaften zu Göttingen_).
